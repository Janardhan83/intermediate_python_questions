## 📌 Project Overview

The **Store Sales Analyzer** is an intermediate-level Python project designed to analyze sales transactions and generate meaningful business insights.

The program processes sales records and performs revenue analysis, quantity analysis, category-level reporting, product ranking, performance evaluation, and contribution analysis.

This project simulates real-world business reporting tasks commonly performed by Data Analysts.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

The project performs the following tasks:

* Calculate product-wise revenue.
* Find the highest revenue-generating product.
* Calculate total quantity sold for each product.
* Identify the best-selling product.
* Generate category-wise revenue summaries.
* Assign performance levels to products.
* Calculate revenue contribution percentages.
* Generate a complete business report.

---

# 🛠 Project Workflow

## Step 1: Product Revenue Analysis

Calculate total revenue generated by each product.

Formula:

```text
Revenue = Quantity Sold × Price Per Unit
```

Example:

```text
Laptop -> 150000
Mobile -> 100000
Shirt -> 10000
```

Concepts Used:

* Dictionary Aggregation
* Arithmetic Operations
* Data Processing

---

## Step 2: Highest Revenue Product

Identify the product generating the highest revenue.

Example:

```text
Laptop -> 150000
```

Concepts Used:

* Maximum Value Search
* Dictionary Traversal

---

## Step 3: Quantity Sold Analysis

Calculate the total quantity sold for each product.

Example:

```text
Laptop -> 3
Rice -> 30
```

Concepts Used:

* Aggregation Logic
* Counting Techniques

---

## Step 4: Best-Selling Product

Determine the product with the highest quantity sold.

Example:

```text
Rice -> 30 Units
```

Concepts Used:

* Ranking
* Maximum Value Identification

---

## Step 5: Category Revenue Analysis

Calculate revenue generated by each category.

Example:

```text
Electronics -> 250000
Clothing -> 22000
Grocery -> 3750
```

Concepts Used:

* Grouping Data
* Category-Based Analysis

---

## Step 6: Product Performance Evaluation

Assign performance levels based on total revenue.

Rules:

| Revenue  | Performance |
| -------- | ----------- |
| ≥ 100000 | Excellent   |
| ≥ 50000  | Good        |
| ≥ 10000  | Average     |
| < 10000  | Low         |

---

## Step 7: Revenue Contribution Analysis

Calculate each product's contribution to total store revenue.

Formula:

```text
(Product Revenue / Total Revenue) × 100
```

Example:

```text
Laptop -> 54.13%
Mobile -> 36.08%
```

Concepts Used:

* Percentage Calculations
* Business Analytics

---

## Step 8: Final Business Report

Generate a complete sales report including:

* Product Revenue
* Quantity Sold
* Product Performance
* Revenue Contribution
* Highest Revenue Product
* Best-Selling Product
* Category Revenue Summary
